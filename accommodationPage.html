Choose Your Ideal Room

At Organo Hotel & Suites, we offer a range of elegant and comfortable rooms to suit your needs:

  • Standard Room: Cozy and comfortable, with all the essentials for a pleasant stay. This is the starting room category in our hotel, designed for guests who value simplicity and comfort. We have one Standard Rooms on each floor, making it our most accessible and budget-friendly option. It’s the perfect choice for short stays and everyday travel needs. ₦40,000 / Night
  • Deluxe Room: Extra space and premium amenities for a touch of elegance. Larger than our Standard Room, this category offers added comfort and sophistication. Designed for guests who enjoy a little more luxury, it features a private bathtub, enhanced furnishings, and a more spacious layout — ideal for relaxation after a long day. ₦45,000 / Night
  • Luxury Suite: The pinnacle of opulence, featuring top-tier amenities and refined living spaces. This is our most prestigious room and the one we are truly proud of. It offers a separate living room, a larger luxury bathtub, and two private toilets, delivering unmatched comfort, privacy, and elegance. Thoughtfully designed for guests who expect nothing but the best, this room represents the highest standard of our hospitality. ₦65,000 / Night
